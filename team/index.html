Director

Sean H. Wang

Founder and Director, Wang Flight Systems Research Division LLC

Sean has been building Wang Flight Systems - its engineering foundations, its legal architecture, and its research program - since before any of its entities existed on paper. The propulsion architecture, the thermal protection system concept, the corporate structure, and the operating agreements are all his original work. The dream of hypersonic civilian transport, of ordinary people flying anywhere on Earth in under three hours at economy class prices, is what started all of it. Everything built since has been in service of that.
