Who We Are

Wang Flight Systems is organized as four independent limited liability companies, each responsible for a distinct phase of bringing hypersonic civilian transport to market.

Wang Flight Systems Research Division LLC

The intellectual engine of the program. Every publication, every university collaboration, every federal grant, and every airline development agreement flows through this entity. When airlines and government agencies write checks to advance hypersonic civilian transport, this is who receives them.

Wang Flight Systems Manufacturing Division LLC

The entity that turns validated technology into deliverable aircraft. Intellectual property licensed from the Research Division becomes certified hardware here. When purchase agreements are exercised and delivery slots are confirmed, this is who builds and sells the aircraft. Not yet incorporated.

Wang Flight Systems Certification & Airworthiness LLC

The entity that makes the aircraft legal to fly. FAA certification programs, airworthiness documentation, and the regulatory relationships that govern civil aviation authority approval worldwide are owned and managed here. No aircraft enters service without this division's sign-off. Not yet incorporated.

Wang Flight Systems MRO Division LLC

The entity that protects the asset value of every aircraft delivered. Maintenance programs, component lifecycle management, and fleet-wide airworthiness from first flight through end of service are executed here. Airlines buying Wang Flight Systems aircraft are buying a support structure, not just a vehicle. Not yet incorporated.

Open Access Commitment

Wang Flight Systems does not file patents. Every fundamental scientific result produced by this program is committed to the public literature. Draft manuscripts are made available on this website at the time of journal publication, without paywalls and without delay. The science that makes hypersonic civilian transport possible belongs to everyone.
